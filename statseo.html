FAQs

What are the limitations of StatSEO?
As with any statistical analysis there are limitations- our use of 95% confidence interval implies by definition at least a 5% rate of erroneous identification (i.e. 1 in 20).
What's the difference between Good, OK and Bad?
In all the pie charts shown here, ‘bad’ refers to links or pages that are likely to suffer detrimental effect, ‘OK’ refers to those that are neutral without any significant effect seen and ‘good’ refers to link or pages with feature values assessed to have positive influence on visitor traffic.
